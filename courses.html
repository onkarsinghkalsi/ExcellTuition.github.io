COURSES OFFERED

1-year classroom programme for 10+1

EXCELL TUITION CENTRE has specially designed this course for the students presently studying in Class +1. This module helps students to prepare to ascertain their success in Board exams with dashing colours. This programme fosters the students in a scientific manner with the best of updated study material, highly dedicated and experienced faculty. Regular Tests, doubt clearing sessions, etc. are some of the distinguishing features of this programme.

Salient Features:

  • Small batch size.
  • Study material containing Notes, Test Papers and Assignments.
  • Classroom coaching 6 days a week.

1-year classroom programme for 10+2

This programme has been specially designed for the students presently studying in Class +2. This module not only helps students to prepare for ascertaining their success in Board exams with very good marks, but also equips them with the perfect know-how of the technicalities of the various Entrance Examinations like CBSE-PMT, AIIMS, AFMC, State PMTs, IIT-JEE and AIEEE. This programme fosters the students in a scientific manner with the best of updated Study Material, highly dedicated and experienced faculty.

Salient Features:

  • Small batch size.
  • Study material containing Notes, Test Papers and Assignments.
  • Classroom coaching 6 days a week.

WEEKEND COMPETITION BATCH (for +1 / +2 studying students)

This Programme is for the students studying in +1/ +2, who have covered the syllabus of +1/ +2 thoroughly and want to prepare for Career Oriented Entrance Examinations. In this Programme while studying in +1/ +2, a student can prepare for Medical / Engineering entrance tests on Saturdays and Sundays. Duration of the course is from April to December.

Crash Courses

Crash courses are of about 60 days. The students of 10+1/10+2 can join in a fast track program for the preparation of various competitions. The course starts in the month of March after the 10+2 exams are over. Students are provided with the best of the updated study material. Special doubt clearance sessions are held during the course.
